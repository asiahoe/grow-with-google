Siamese Cat

The Siamese cat is one of the first distinctly recognized breeds of Asian cat.

  • Scientific Name: Felis Silvestrius
  • Family: Felidae
  • Genus: Felis
  • Order: Carnivora

Derived from the Wichianmat landrace, one of several varieties of cat native to Thailand (formerly known as Siam), the Siamese became one of the most popular breeds in Europe and North America in the 19th century.
